CORE FEATURES

Display anything quick and easy

Cost-effective and Scalable

As an open-source platform, Commerseum is not only free to use but also benefits from a thriving community of developers and contributors. This ensures continuous improvements, regular updates, and the latest features without the excessive costs associated with proprietary systems. With Commerseum, you can scale your digital signage network without breaking the bank.

Customizable and Flexible

Commerseum's modular architecture and extensive customization options allow you to tailor your digital signage to your unique needs. Choose from a wide array of pre-built templates and widgets or create your own from scratch, using our user-friendly design tools. With OpenSign, you have full creative control to design the perfect display for your brand.

Easy Integration and Management

Commerseum is designed to work seamlessly with popular content management systems, social media platforms, and data sources, allowing you to easily integrate and manage your digital signage content. Our intuitive dashboard and powerful content scheduling features ensure that your displays always stay fresh and relevant.

Cross-platform Compatibility

Commerseum is built on web technologies, making it compatible with a broad range of devices and operating systems. Whether you're using Linux, Windows, macOS, or Android, OpenSign has you covered. Deploy your digital signage on anything from large LED screens to compact Raspberry Pi devices without compromising performance
